ISRO apprentice

by Chetna Bopche
December 2, 2020

ISRO provides internship programs for both undergraduate and postgraduate students. Indian Space Research Organization has many centers across the countries which contributes in different manner. ISRO centers to work in are ISAC (Bangalore) and SAC (Ahmedabad). Other than these NRSC (Hyderabad), VSSC (Thiruvanathapuram) etc. also provides some internship projects.

In the most of the observed cases, they permit an internship or in-house training for their projects to final year students of B. Tech/B.E. and to those who are pursuing master's in recognized institutes. Generally, ISRO centers receive many applications from various institutes to permit their distinguished students to do an internship. They review their profiles and based on the profiles of the students, their interests for research areas and according to their project requirements they allow such internship.

ISRO generally prefers Post Graduate students as trainees. So, for undergraduate students it is quite difficult to get an internship and generally relatives of ISRO employees and/or the local students (same state in which the center is situated) are preferred.

When applying for internship, the application should be routed through your college administration, as individual applications are not entertained. It is better if you are studying in recognized institute, as chances are better obviously. Even if not, your institute (i.e.your T&P cell or your department chair) can send a request letter. Usually the toppers of the institute or respected department are allowed to do projects. So, one who desires to do internship at ISRO centers should be a topper/should have excellent GPA. They may allow one if he has done some very good projects in a field or have some experience or have some publications in a research area.

It is also possible in this cases that based on the request of your institute or your professor, you may be called upon for a meeting to discuss your past work and their project requirements and then you may be allowed. The complete internship request can be send/mailed to HR department of center or to the director office atleast 3-4 months before the internship period.
